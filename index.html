About Streetwise Advisors

Streetwise Advisors is a management consulting firm based in Accra, Ghana, offering expert services in corporate finance, strategic planning, digital transformation, cybersecurity, and investment advisory. Founded in 2020, the firm is led by Managing Partner Augustine Baidoo and a team of seasoned professionals. Streetwise delivers tailored, innovative solutions that drive growth and long-term success, with a strong focus on integrity, excellence, and client-centric service.
