About Streetwise Advisors

Streetwise Advisors is a management consulting firm based in Accra, Ghana, specializing in corporate finance, strategic business planning, digital transformation, cybersecurity, and investment advisory services. Established in November 2020, our mission is to deliver tailored solutions that drive growth and innovation for our clients.​

Our Services

  • Corporate Finance: We offer financial structuring, capital raising, and mergers and acquisitions advisory to optimize our clients' financial performance.​
  • Strategic Business Planning: Our team assists organizations in developing comprehensive strategies, including business reviews and audits, to achieve long-term objectives.​
  • Digital Transformation: Leveraging AI and machine learning, we help businesses streamline operations and enhance efficiency.​
  • Cybersecurity: We provide training and awareness programs to strengthen organizations' defenses against cyber threats.​
  • Investment Advisory: Our services encompass purchasing investment instruments for clients and acting as arrangers for investment deals.​

Our Team

Led by Managing Partner Augustine Baidoo, our team comprises seasoned professionals with extensive experience in various industries. We are committed to delivering exceptional service and building lasting relationships with our clients.​
